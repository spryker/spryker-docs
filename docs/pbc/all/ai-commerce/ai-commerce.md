AI Commerce is a collection of AI-powered storefront features built on top of the `AiFoundation` abstraction layer. It lets you integrate AI capabilities into commerce workflows without coupling your project to a specific AI provider.

## Features

| FEATURE | DESCRIPTION |
|---------|-------------|
| [Visual Add to Cart](/docs/pbc/all/ai-commerce/visual-add-to-cart/visual-add-to-cart.html) | Lets buyers upload a product image on the Quick Order page to automatically recognize products and populate the order form. |
