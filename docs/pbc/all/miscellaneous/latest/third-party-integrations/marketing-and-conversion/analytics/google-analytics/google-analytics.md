## Partner Information

[ABOUT GOOGLE ANALYTICS](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/)

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is Google's analytics platform for measuring traffic and user engagement across websites and apps. It uses an event-based data model to provide flexible reporting and integrates with Google's advertising and marketing products.
